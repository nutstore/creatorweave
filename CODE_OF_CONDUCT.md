# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

1. Be respectful and constructive.
2. Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
3. Focus feedback on ideas, code, and behavior.
4. Be open to different viewpoints and experiences.

## Unacceptable Behavior

1. Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
2. Discriminatory or hateful language.
3. Publishing private information without consent.
4. Repeated disruptive behavior after warning.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.
They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and PRs that violate this policy.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it through the private channel in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and community channels.
